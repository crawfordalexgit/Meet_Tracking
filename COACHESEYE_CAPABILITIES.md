# COACHESEYE | STRATEGIC INTELLIGENCE SUITE
## Executive Capabilities & Operational Oversight Manual

CoachesEye is a high-fidelity, data-driven command center engineered for Tonbridge Swimming Club. It transforms raw competitive data into actionable strategic intelligence, providing the coaching team with definitive oversight of athlete development and club-wide performance health.

---

### 1. STRATEGIC COCKPIT (EXECUTIVE OVERSIGHT)
The primary command center for club-wide performance auditing.
*   **Global Club Health**: A weighted aggregate stability score (0-100%) synthesizing attendance, volume, and performance velocity.
*   **Dynamic Strategic Briefing**: A data-driven narrative that automatically summarizes club performance, elite achievements, and operational risks.
*   **Operational Risk Assessment**: Automated identification of compliance gaps and training volume deficits, highlighted with executive intensity.
*   **Ranking Density Gap Analysis**: Visual tracking of elite-tier penetration (National/Regional/County) and identification of competitive bottlenecks.

### 2. PERFORMANCE DNA (ATHLETE ANALYTICS)
Granular, high-fidelity tracking of individual athlete progression.
*   **Individual Performance Profiles**: Deep-dive analytics featuring peak standards, recent form, and season-long PB conversion.
*   **WA Points Velocity (⚡)**: Real-time calculation of performance acceleration versus stagnation relative to seasonal baselines.
*   **PB Conversion Meter**: A kinetic visualizer of technical efficiency—tracking the ratio of race entries to Personal Bests.
*   **Stroke-Specific Benchmarking**: Automated comparison against Kent (County) and South East (Regional) automatic qualifying standards.

### 3. TACTICAL LEADERBOARDS (TALENT RECOGNITION)
Instrumentation for identifying the club's elite ceiling and growth momentum.
*   **Elite Performance Leaderboard**: Ranks the Top 5 athletes by their absolute World Aquatics Peak Standard.
*   **Most Improved (Tactical Acceleration)**: Identifies athletes with the highest growth velocity in WA point averages.
*   **Achievement DNA Tracking**: Categorizes athletes into National (Top 40), Regional (Top 30), and County (Top 10) success tiers.

### 4. OPERATIONAL REGISTRIES (DATABASE MANAGEMENT)
Centralized indices of the club's performance assets.
*   **Squad Registry**: A high-fidelity audit of all competitive pathways, featuring aggregate health, training consistency, and aerobic volume metrics.
*   **Athlete Registry**: A searchable, interactive database of all Tonbridge swimmers, providing instant access to age, squad, and peak performance data.
*   **Meet Intelligence Hub**: A centralized portal for tracking all competitive fixtures, entries, and historical results.

### 5. SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE & INTEGRATION
The technical engine powering the CoachesEye intelligence.
*   **Real-time Performance Sensors**: Live data synchronization via Supabase for attendance, training logs, and result tracking.
*   **Rankings Scraper Engine**: Automated retrieval of regional and national rankings to maintain up-to-date competitive benchmarks.
*   **Tactical Intelligence Guide (INTEL)**: An integrated, globally accessible help system defining all methodologies and KPI logic.
*   **Zero-Gloss Narrative Standard**: A professional briefing standard that adheres strictly to data-driven conclusions, eliminating speculative terminology.
